Setting up 3rd party permissions

Setting up 3rd party permissions

Third-party permission to the PayPal Business Account for accessing PayPal-related payment information is needed for integrating PayPal into an online shop.

Step 1

If no PayPal business account exists, a PayPal Business account needs to be registered a PayPal.

Step 2

After successfully registering a Paypal Business account, provide hobex integration specialists with the email address used to register with Paypal. Remember to add the hobex CustomerID when contacting us!

A user name and password assigned by hobex must be used for the automatic setup described in step 3.

These credentials are issued only when a hobex CustomerID exists! The access data is sent in a password-protected file.

Step 3

The following link leads to the login mask

  • Login with the provided username and password

  • Click in the top right corner on the profile icon and select "my profile"

  • Click on the field "Paypal"

  • Enter the PayPal business email-address and select the country. Press "next"

  • On the next page click "go to PayPal"

  • Login into PayPal and accept that the PayPal account is connected to hobex.

  • After the process was finished, click "back to Qenta"

  • The process is finished if the Paypal MerchantID (eg. NZILK748L85C8) is shown in the hobex Portal. The tab can be closed now and/or use the sign off button. hobex will inform the merchant when the onboarding process was finished
