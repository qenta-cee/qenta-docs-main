Wirecard Checkout Integraton for SAP Business by Design

Wirecard Checkout Integraton for SAP Business by Design

Wirecard Checkout Integration for SAP Business by Design integrates our product "Wirecard Checkout Seamless" (seamlessly integrated PCI DSS compliant solution, more sophisticated than classical payment page) with SAP.

This solution supports mail order/telephone order (MoTo) credit card and debit card transactions and backoffice transactions where all major card brands are supported:

  • Visa

  • MasterCard

  • Amex

  • Diners Club

  • Discover

  • JCB

  • Maestro (only for UK, Ireland, France)

We provide also efficient and proven fraud detection and risk management services which can additionally be used, helping you to decrease levels of fraud and risk whilst not to inhibiting any "good" business, to prevent a negative influence on the overall turnover.

Benefits of this integration to SAP Business by Design:

  • Payments from a single source: PCI DSS compliant payment service solution & acquiring services.

  • Fraud prevention tools can be accessed without further technical adaptation.

  • Acquiring services provided through Wirecard Bank AG, a fully-licensed German

bank, principal member of Visa, MasterCard and JCB and licensed acquirer for Diners Club, Discover, China Union Pay and JCB.

  • Multicurrency banking solutions for payment processing and settlement of funds (including currency conversion).

  • Multichannel solutions: Solutions for all further sales channels including e-Commerce, mobile and point of sale available consolidated via Wirecard.

  • Minimized integration efforts: SAP Business by Design seamlessly pre-integrated with Wirecard payment gateway.

Process overview:

1. on behalf of the customer merchant agent registers credit card/ debit card and stores token to customer’s information in a PCI DSS compliant way

2. after credit card/ debit card is initially registered within SAP BusinessbyDesign credit card/ debit card MoTo transactions can be triggered at any time

3. last step is to trigger the settlement process from within SAP BusinessbyDesign. This is usually done on a daily basis.

Backoffice Operations supported:

  • approve reversal

  • deposit

  • refund

With the technical integration of SAP Business by Design and Wirecard Checkout Seamless, merchants gain access to Wirecard Bank AG’s acquiring solutions for acceptance of credit/ debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex, Diners, Discover, JCB). Wirecard Bank AG is a fully licensed German bank, regulated by German banking authority BaFin and member of the security deposit fund. Further to merchant accounts for card not present acceptance (MID), corporate bank accounts, currency conversion, multicurrency solutions and issuing products are available.
