Wirecard Integration for Shopgate

Wirecard Integration for Shopgate

Shopgate enables online stores to create, maintain and optimize native apps and mobile websites for Android and iOS devices. This is accomplished by integrating Shopgate into your online shop. The Wirecard Integration for Shopgate incorporates our solutions Wirecard Checkout Page and Wirecard Checkout Seamless.

Please note that due to security measures imposed by Shopgate, you may not use the credit card numbers for demo mode using customerId D200001. Instead, you may use customerId D200411 for testing credit card numbers for Wirecard Checkout Page and Wirecard Checkout Seamless.

Supported payment methods

The following payment methods are supported both for Wirecard Checkout Page and Wirecard Checkout Seamless:

  • Credit Card

  • PayPal

  • Sofort.

  • Direct Debit

  • iDEAL - Please contact Shopgate to enable this payment method for Wirecard Checkout Seamless.

If you have questions or problems regarding the implementation, please contact the Shopgate support.

Please note that currently any back-end operations such as deposit or refund need to be performed in the Wirecard Payment Center.

Adding a payment method

To add a payment method, first navigate to the entry "Payment" in the "Settings" menu.

Then click on the select field "Please choose a payment method".

Search for the payment method by entering the name or scroll down to the section "Wirecard (Checkout Page)" or "Wirecard (Checkout Seamless)" and select the payment method you want to add.

Configuring a payment method

After adding a payment method, it needs to be configured. The following parameters need to be set for each payment method in "Settings":

Please fill in your Customer ID, Secret and optionally your Shop ID into the respective fields at the payment method configuration. When configuring the payment method credit card you also need to select the brands you have a contract for.

Customer ID Required Your customer ID you received from Wirecard.

Secret

Required

Your secret you received from Wirecard.

Shop ID

Optional

Unique identifier for your shop. This parameter has to be enabled by Wirecard for your customer ID.

Customization possibilities

If Wirecard Checkout Page is the incorporated solution, it is recommended to have a look at the provided customization possibilities for an optimized design.

If Wirecard Checkout Seamless is the incorporated solution, the intermediate page which displays status information to your consumer during the payment process may be customized in order to better match the design of you online shop. For more information please have a look at the relevant customization possibilities.
