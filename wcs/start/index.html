QMORE Checkout Seamless

QMORE Checkout Seamless

QMORE Checkout Seamless is a sophisticated and secure possibility for seamlessly integrating a payment process into your online shop. There is no need to manage and store sensitive payment data in your online shop, thus you do not need to be PCI compliant, but you can directly integrate all relevant input fields for payments seamlessly into your online shop interface. Thus can customly design every step of the checkout process.

If you are new to e-commerce and first need some basic information especially for defining the checkout process in your online shop, we have compiled for you A Comprehensive Guide to Payment which provides extensive background information and contains the most important milestones summed up in chronological order.

For an overview of this payment solution visit qenta.at.

Benefits and features

  • Ensures PCI DSS standard of the PCI Standard Council for secure payment transactions.

  • Sensitive payment data (e.g. credit card numbers or CVC codes) are entered by your consumers in your HTML forms in your shop and directly transferred to QENTA.

  • Integration respects the design and style guides of your online shop.

  • Customizable payment process to fit your business requirements.

  • Support of many global and local financial service providers.

  • Wide range of optional features and functionalities.

  • Optional integration of a risk management.

  • Dedicated and experienced support teams help you with any issues regarding integration.
