Demo Mode

Demo Mode

The integration of products in the online shop can be checked with a demo mode. There is no communication with the financial service provider but only between the online shop and hobex Service IT. A connection to the Internet is required to gain access to Checkout Page and the payment process is only simulated displaying the financial service provider pages as images and the possibility to select a successful or failed payment process result. The executed payments are not displayed in the Payment Center.
